Aria Rastegar

PhD Researcher

RTG Dimensions of Constructional Space at, the FAU

Welcome to my personal website. I am a PhD researcher in Linguistics with a focus on experimental studies at Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg. My current project investigates how people understand idioms in their second language (L2), and how that understanding is influenced by cross-linguistic correspondences between idioms. In this research, I work with English, German, and Persian idioms.
